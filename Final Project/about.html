About

Jupiter Sport Fishing is a local team of avid sport fishermen. It is comprised of five people Jake, Andrew, Nick, Brian, and AJ. Together the team has won multiple tournaments off the coast of Jupiter and Palm Beach. With the team all attending different colleges they get together over breaks and fish as much as humanly possible. There is nothing better than getting on the water and catching a big game fish. Depending on the severity of the fishing we bring other friends out with us and even just cruise the coast for a little bit of fun.

 

Jake Eisenberg | TC 349
